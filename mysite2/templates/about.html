About the Best Practices Movie Scheduler

The Best Practices Movie Scheduler uses a two-tier approach to solving the problem of movie scheduling.

In the first tier, we generate the priority score for each movie from the information we collected through your survey. Using the calculated priority score, we compute the number of showings per movie, wherein the movie with the maximum score is allotted maximum screening.

In the second tier, we deal with our main scheduling problem, wherein we plot the movies with a higher priority score to prime showings. This way we satisty the demand of the audience, ensure success of the movies and gain you some more profit!
